Billing & Subscription

Startup Cohort Discount

Follow these steps to redeem your startup coupon and subscribe to ThriveStack.

Eligibility

Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) less than $100K
Applicable only on the Growth Monthly plan

Before you begin

Prerequisites

Required Sign up on ThriveStack at app.thrivestack.ai
Required Coupon code — write to support@thrivestack.ai with your subscription ID from Settings → Payments/Billing to get yours
Required A valid card to add during checkout

1 Go to Settings

Click Settings from the bottom of the left navigation panel.

2 Click "Manage Subscription"

On the Payments/Billing page, click Manage Subscription to view available plans.

3 Select the Growth Plan

Review the available plans and click Choose Growth to select the Growth plan.

4 Apply a Coupon (optional)

On the Review Your Subscription page, enter your coupon code in the Have a coupon code? field and click Apply.

5 Continue to Checkout

Click Continue to Checkout and add your card details to complete the payment.

For any queries, write to support@thrivestack.ai
