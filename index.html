Getting Started with ThriveStack

Growth Intelligence

ThriveStack's Growth Intelligence engine unifies signals across your entire go-to-market stack to give you a single, actionable view of growth. Instead of relying on fragmented data silos, our platform brings everything together seamlessly.

Overview

Growth Intelligence combines data from marketing, sales, product, billing, and customer success into correlated signals. Instead of switching between 5+ tools, your team sees one coherent picture of how your business is growing.

Unified View

Stop context switching. See all customer actions in one timeline.

Real-time Sync

Data is synced instantly across your CRM, helpdesk, and billing platforms.

Unified dashboard

The dashboard surfaces key metrics across every stage of your funnel — from first touch through expansion. Filter by segment, cohort, time range, or custom properties to drill into the signals that matter most.

Signal correlation

ThriveStack automatically links related signals across all your tools. For example, it connects marketing website visitors to SaaS product telemetry, Stripe billing events, HubSpot CRM stages, and customer success support interactions — building a complete picture of each account's journey.

AI-powered insights

The AI layer continuously analyzes your signals to surface opportunities and risks. It detects patterns like:

  • Accounts likely to expand Identify up-sell opportunities automatically based on usage spikes and feature adoption.
  • Conversion bottlenecks See exactly where users drop off in your funnel and get recommendations to fix it.
  • Early churn indicators Proactively reach out before it's too late when engagement drops.

Then, the engine recommends specific actions to drive growth and retention, pushing tasks directly to your teams' preferred tools.
