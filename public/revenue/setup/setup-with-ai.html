How does it work?

Getting accurate MRR, ARR, and churn metrics traditionally requires wiring together your billing system, a data warehouse, and a BI tool — weeks of engineering work. ThriveStack Revenue Intelligence changes that: paste one prompt into your AI coding assistant and it instruments your billing integration in minutes, joining revenue data with product usage at the account level automatically.
