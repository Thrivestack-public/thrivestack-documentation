Step-by-step breakdown

Each step in the cycle — who does what, and what data moves.

1
Google Ads AcmeLab
AcmeLab launches a Google Ads campaign

AcmeLab's marketing team creates campaigns in Google Ads targeting specific keywords, audiences, or industries. Each ad has a destination URL on AcmeLab's marketing website.

2
Visitor Google Ads
A visitor sees the ad and clicks it

Google Ads serves the ad to a relevant user. When the user clicks, Google appends a unique gclid (Google Click ID) parameter to the landing page URL. This ID is the fingerprint that ties the entire journey back to the original ad click.

3
ThriveStack
ThriveStack captures the click and visitor identity

ThriveStack's script (already installed on AcmeLab's marketing site) reads the gclid and UTM parameters from the URL. It creates an anonymous visitor profile and stores the ad attribution data, even before the visitor creates an account.

4
Visitor AcmeLab
Visitor signs up for AcmeLab

The visitor navigates to AcmeLab's product, creates an account, and begins a trial. At the moment of signup, ThriveStack links the new user identity to the original anonymous visitor profile — completing the first attribution match: this user came from that specific ad click.

5
Visitor AcmeLab
Visitor converts to a paid customer

The user upgrades to a paid pricing plan on AcmeLab. ThriveStack captures the payment event — including the plan name, MRR value, and billing currency — and ties it to the same attribution chain that started with the Google Ads click.

6
ThriveStack
ThriveStack enriches with user & company-level data

ThriveStack enriches the conversion record with both user-level and company-level information — job title, company name, industry, employee count, revenue range, and product usage signals. This context transforms a raw click into a rich conversion profile.

7
ThriveStack Google Ads
ThriveStack sends the enriched conversion back to Google Ads

Using the Google Ads Offline Conversions API, ThriveStack sends the full enriched record — matched via the original gclid — back to Google Ads. This includes both the conversion event (signup, paid) and the company-level context that gives Google the signal quality it needs to optimize effectively.

8
Google Ads AcmeLab
Google Ads optimizes future campaigns for AcmeLab

With real conversion data in place, Google's Smart Bidding can now optimize for the outcomes that actually matter to AcmeLab — paid signups, not just clicks or form fills. It builds lookalike audiences from the company profiles, raises bids for high-intent segments, and reduces spend on clicks that never convert.

What AcmeLab unlocks

Once the integration is live, AcmeLab gets capabilities that no standard Google Ads setup can provide.

Revenue-based bidding

Google's Smart Bidding optimizes for actual paid conversions instead of clicks or form submissions.

Company-level lookalikes

Google builds lookalike audiences from real paying customers — including company size, industry, and role.

True ROI visibility

See exactly which campaigns, ad groups, and keywords drove MRR — not just which ones drove traffic.

Self-improving campaigns

Every new conversion makes the next campaign smarter. The feedback loop compounds over time without manual tuning.

Wasted spend elimination

Suppress ads for audiences that never convert. Stop paying for clicks from companies that are too small, too large, or wrong industry.

Full-journey attribution
