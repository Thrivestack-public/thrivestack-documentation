Marketing Intelligence

Introduction

Start here to set up marketing signal collection and attribution.

What you need

Someone who can deploy to the marketing website Access to the marketing site codebase or CMS

ThriveStack Marketing Analytics goes beyond page views and traffic summaries. It helps your team connect marketing efforts to product behavior and revenue outcomes, so you can decide where to invest with confidence.

  • Channel Performance
  • Touchpoints to Acquisition
  • Time to Acquisition
  • Lead Quality
  • Multi-Touch Attribution Modeling
  • Revenue Impact Tracking
  • Campaign Performance Analytics

What will you achieve? 🎆

Traditional Marketing Analytics tools typically just show you vanity metrics like Traffic, Page Visits, and Visitor Trends — and most of that is free with tools like Google Analytics. ThriveStack goes far beyond that. It gives you full‑journey correlation by stitching together Marketing touchpoints, Product usage, Account behavior, and Billing events.
