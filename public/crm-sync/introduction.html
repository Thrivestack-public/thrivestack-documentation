Prerequisites

  • A HubSpot account — Free tier or above
  • Admin-level access to your HubSpot portal to authorize the OAuth connection
  • A ThriveStack account — sign up free
  • For Deals: HubSpot Sales Hub (any paid tier). For Tickets: HubSpot Service Hub (any paid tier)

How the sync works

Both directions use incremental sync — only records that changed since the last run are transferred. This keeps sync fast and your data fresh without re-importing everything every day.

Syncs run on a daily schedule automatically. You can also trigger an immediate sync at any time from the ⋯ menu on your connection card.
