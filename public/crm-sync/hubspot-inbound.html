CRM Sync · HubSpot

HubSpot → ThriveStack

Import companies, contacts, deals, tickets and engagement history from HubSpot. Every account in ThriveStack shows its CRM context alongside product usage.

What you need

A HubSpot account with at least one company or contact record Admin-level HubSpot access to authorize the OAuth connection Sales Hub for deals · Service Hub for tickets (any paid tier)

What gets imported

The following objects are synced from HubSpot into ThriveStack on an incremental daily schedule. All standard properties are imported — plus any custom properties you've created in your portal.

Object Default What's included
Companies On Domain, name, industry, revenue, employee count, lifecycle stage, owner, all custom properties
Contacts On Name, email, job title, lifecycle stage, all custom properties
Deals On Name, amount, stage, pipeline, close date, owner. Requires company association to be set in HubSpot.
Tickets On Subject, priority, status, source type (email/chat/form/phone). Requires company association.
Calls On Title, notes, duration, direction (inbound/outbound)
Meetings On Title, notes, start time
Notes On Note body
Tasks On Subject, status, priority, due date
Emails Off One-to-one sales email logs. Off by default — large portals can have millions of records. Enable only if your team specifically needs email history.

How to set up

1
Open the CRM sync wizard /analyze/onboarding/crm

Go to Analyze → Onboarding → CRM and click Add Connection.

2
Select "Inbound" direction

On the direction step choose HubSpot → ThriveStack.

3
Authorize with HubSpot

Click the HubSpot tile then Authorize with HubSpot. You'll be redirected to HubSpot's OAuth consent page — sign in with an account that has Super Admin or CRM Admin access and click Connect app. You'll be returned to ThriveStack automatically.

4
Choose which objects to import

Toggle on the objects you want. All objects except Emails are on by default. Enable Emails only if your team specifically needs one-to-one sales email history — large portals can have millions of records.

5
Review and activate

Review the summary and click Create Connection. The first full sync typically finishes in 5–30 minutes. All subsequent syncs are incremental.

Where to find imported data

Go to Analyze → Accounts, open any account, and look for the HubSpot panel. ThriveStack matches accounts to HubSpot companies by domain. You'll see four tabs:

  • Properties — All HubSpot company properties including custom fields.
  • Deals — Open and closed deals with stage, amount, close date and owner. Shows open pipeline total and closed-won total at a glance.
  • Tickets — Support tickets with priority, stage and source channel.
  • Engagements — Full activity timeline — calls, meetings, notes and tasks sorted by most recent. Filter by type.

Sync schedule

Run Mode Notes
Daily at 02:00 UTC Incremental Only records modified since the last run are fetched. New records are appended; updated records overwrite the previous version.
Manual trigger Incremental Click ⋯ → Trigger Sync on your connection card to run an immediate sync without waiting for the daily schedule.
First sync Full refresh The first run after connecting imports all historical records. Takes 5 min (small portal) to several hours (100k+ records). All subsequent runs are incremental.

Refreshing an existing connection

If you connected HubSpot before deals and engagements were supported, a banner will appear on the CRM page. Two scenarios:

  • Re-authorize — Your OAuth token is missing required permissions. Click the button, approve on HubSpot, and your connection upgrades automatically with all new data streams in one step. No data is lost.
  • Enable now — Your token already has the right permissions but the sync pipeline is missing the new streams. One click, no redirect, done in seconds.

Troubleshooting

Deals or tickets not showing in account view

Each deal or ticket must be associated with a company record in HubSpot for it to appear on the correct account in ThriveStack. Go to your HubSpot records and verify the Company association is populated.

No data after 30 minutes

The first sync can take longer for large portals. Check the Last synced timestamp on your connection card. If it shows an error use ⋯ → Trigger Sync to retry.

Authorization failed: redirect URL doesn't match

Ensure https://app.thrivestack.ai/analyze/onboarding/crm is listed in the Redirect URLs in your HubSpot app settings. Changes can take 2–5 minutes to propagate.

HubSpot properties show raw IDs instead of names

Deal stages, owner names and pipeline IDs are stored as raw identifiers in HubSpot's API (e.g. closedwon, 12345678). Human-readable label resolution is on the roadmap.

If this doesn't resolve your issue or you're running into a problem we haven't covered, email us at support@thrivestack.ai and we'll help you.
