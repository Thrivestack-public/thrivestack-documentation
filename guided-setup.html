Guided Setup

Overview

A high-level overview of how the guided setup with ThriveStack works — how many sessions each track takes, who to bring to each one, and what you'll have set up by the end.

ThriveStack setup is structured into tracks — Marketing Analytics, Product Analytics, and more. Each track is broken into focused sessions with a clear owner, a defined scope, and a specific outcome. Use this page to understand the full picture before you begin, and to align your team on who needs to be involved and when.

Marketing Analytics

Track visitors, attribute signups, and connect marketing channels to product outcomes.

1 session ~1–2 hrs
Who to bring
Developer or marketer with CMS access Access to the marketing site or codebase
Session plan
Session 1 Install & Verify ~1–2 hrs
Install the tracking snippet on your marketing site, verify page view and conversion events, and confirm attribution is working end to end.
What you unlock
Which channels drive signups and free trials How leads move from marketing touch to signup Full visitor-to-customer attribution Marketing-to-product journey correlation
View full session guide

Product Analytics

Instrument your product, map feature usage, and track activation journeys end to end.

4 sessions ~8–12 hrs
Who to bring
Backend engineer Frontend engineer Product manager
Session plan
Session 1 User & Group Instrumentation ~2–3 hrs
Wire up identify and group calls at signup, login, and account creation so every user and workspace is tracked from day one.
Session 2 Feature Usage Instrumentation ~2–3 hrs
Add track calls to key product features and user interactions — the events that reveal how people actually use your product.
Session 3 Activation Journey Setup ~2–3 hrs
Map your onboarding funnel, define activation milestones, and configure the journey from signup to first value.
Session 4 End-to-End Validation ~1 hr
Validate reports and account lists, then walk through diagnostics — live events, audit logs, and debugging tools for the future.
What you unlock
Feature adoption and stickiness by cohort Activation rates and funnel drop-off visibility Retention and early churn signals Account-level journey from signup to active user
View full session guide

Revenue Analytics

Connect your billing system and see investor-grade revenue reports, account-level details, channel performance, and payment failures.

1 session ~60 min
Who to bring
Engineer with billing codebase access Stripe or Chargebee admin Billing repo + credentials
Session plan
Session 1 Connect & Verify ~60 min
Authorize the integration, validate data sync, and confirm revenue metrics are flowing into ThriveStack.
Session 1 Discovery & Recommendations ~1–2 hrs
Walk through your billing system, understand the data model, and map out the API calls needed to surface revenue data in ThriveStack.
Session 2 Implementation & Validation ~1–2 hrs
Review the completed integration, validate revenue reports against billing data, and confirm everything is accurate.
What you unlock
Investor-grade revenue reports (MRR, ARR, churn) Account-level revenue details Channel performance by revenue Payment failure analysis
View full session guide

CRM Sync

Connect your CRM to bring sales context into ThriveStack and push product signals back to your sales team.

2 sessions ~2–3 hrs
Who to bring
HubSpot admin Sales ops or RevOps manager HubSpot workflow access
Session plan
Session 1 HubSpot → ThriveStack ~1 hr
Sync HubSpot contacts, companies, and deals into ThriveStack so your sales-led signals show up alongside product data.
Session 2 ThriveStack → HubSpot ~1–2 hrs
Push product signals — feature adoption, activation status, health scores — into HubSpot, and set up automation workflows to act on them.
What you unlock
Sales-led signals and deal context in ThriveStack Product adoption signals in HubSpot Automated workflows triggered by product activity Full sales-to-product journey visibility
View full session guide

Before the first session

1
Create your ThriveStack account Sign up at app.thrivestack.ai and retrieve your API key from Settings → API Keys.
2
Decide which tracks to run Marketing Analytics and Product Analytics can run in parallel or sequentially. Most teams start with Marketing Analytics (1 session) while Product Analytics is scheduled over 1–2 weeks.
3
Identify the right people Use the "Who to bring" lists above to confirm who needs to join each session. Blocked engineers are the most common reason sessions slip — pre-book their calendars.
4
Book time with the ThriveStack team Schedule your first session — the ThriveStack team walks through the setup with you live.
