Guided Onboarding

Setup with ThriveStack Team

Our team sets up ThriveStack with you — live, in your codebase. Below is a guide on who to bring to each session so we can get you fully instrumented without back-and-forth.

Marketing Analytics

~30 min

Visitor identification, UTM attribution, and source tracking across your marketing site.

Who to bring

Engineer or site owner Can deploy changes to the marketing website

What to have ready

Marketing website codebase
Or CMS access (Webflow, WordPress, etc.)
Setup with AI · Setup Manually

Product Analytics

~60 min

Full product telemetry — signups, logins, feature usage, and the complete customer journey.

Who to bring

Engineer(s) Access to all product codebases

What to have ready

One or more repos covering your user lifecycle and product features
Ability to commit and push changes during the session
Setup with AI · Setup Manually

Revenue Analytics

~60 min

MRR, ARR, churn, expansion, and contraction — at the account level, joined with product usage.

Who to bring

Engineer Access to the backend billing codebase
Revenue / Billing admin Admin access to Stripe or Chargebee

What to have ready

Repo where your billing and subscription logic lives
Stripe or Chargebee account access (test + production)
Setup with AI · Setup Manually

CRM Sync

~45 min

Product and revenue data synced into your HubSpot accounts and contacts — no manual exports.

Who to bring

HubSpot admin Admin access to the HubSpot account

What to have ready

HubSpot admin credentials
Properties you want synced (optional but helpful)
Setup with AI · Setup Manually
