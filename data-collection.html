Contextual Definitions of "You"

Depending on the context, “you” might be an End Customer, End User, Representative, or Visitor:

End Users. When you use an End User Service, such as saving a payment method with Link, for personal use we refer to you as an “End User.”

End Customers. When you are not directly transacting with ThriveStack, but we receive your Personal Data to provide Services to a Business User, including when you make a purchase from a Business User on a Stripe Checkout page or receive payments or other services from a Business User, we refer to you as an “End Customer.”

Representatives. When you are acting on behalf of an existing or potential Business User—perhaps as a company founder, account administrator for a Business User, or a recipient of an employee credit card from a Business User via Stripe Issuing—we refer to you as a “Representative.”

Visitors. When you interact with ThriveStack by visiting a Site without being logged into a ThriveStack account, or when your interaction with ThriveStack does not involve you being an End User, End Customer, or Representative.

Revenue Intelligence

Collects and aggregates Revenue data. For more details, refer to the API References:

  • Subscriptions
  • Subscription Items
  • Invoices (including Taxes)
  • Transactions

Includes name, email address, Transaction amount, Currency, Payment method.

No Credit card or bank information is collected.

All data is Readonly and can never be written/updated to your Billing systems (Stripe, Chargebee, and other connectors).
